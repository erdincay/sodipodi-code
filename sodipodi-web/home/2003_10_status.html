New Patch Process

We've adopted a slightly more formalized process for accepting and integrating patches. We encourage patches to be stored into the Sodipodi patch manager on SourceForge, and encourage people to test out the patches and indicate success or issues encountered. These will be integrated together into a branch called 'hydra_october'.

We're hopeful this new process will both encourage folks to submit more patches and provide users with a convenient way to get all the most recent patches in one package.

"Sodipodi Users Manual" (SUM) Project

Cidric Gemy is organizing writing of a manual for users of Sodipodi. This will bring together several of the disparate documents into a single product for translation and rendering into printable (PDF) form. He will be providing a French version first. Others have indicated interest in helping with English and German translations. More volunteers to write, translate, and create images are *quite* welcome. Stay tuned for more information, or check out the Sodipodi Forums.
