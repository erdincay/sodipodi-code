Below is the Sodipodi International Flag and Civic Heraldry Collection. SVG versions of flags from different countries from around the world are provided for public use. Note that these may not be authoritatively correct (colors, dimensions, etc.) but if you know the correct RGB triplets, feel free to send a corrected version to the Sodipodi web administors. Also see http://flagspot.net/ for details about flags around the world and http://www.ngw.nl/ for civic heraldry.

These images may be freely reused, limited only by the normal regulations of the nation in question.
