Artist Info: Frustrated by too many years of programming, Burton decided to go through his old sketchbooks and try to figure out how to make pretty pictures instead of just admiring them. After experimenting with a variety of programs, he chose Sodipodi as his main illustration tool, and uses gimp for final image compositing and manipulation. For a complete gallery, other works and contact info please go to his home page at http://kruhftwerk.dyndns.org.
