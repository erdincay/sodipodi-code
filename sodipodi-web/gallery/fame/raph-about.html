Raph Levien

Personal info

The personal homepage of Raph is at www.levien.com where you can find both material about his life as well as his activities, coding mostly.
Alternatively, Raph has an account at www.advogato.org, where he keeps a pretty up-to date diary. He also has a really impressive certification list.
Raph is a member of the board of directors of the GNOME foundation.

Contributions

As mentioned in the short note, Raph Levien is the author of Gill. When Lauris felt the need for a vector-drawing application, he started from Gills codebase. To my knowledge, the devlopment of Gill is frozen.
On a larger scale, Raph is doing a lot of work on Ghostscript which is a set of software that allows manipulating of Postscript and PDF documents and interaction with various printers.
His other contributions go to packages like libart and gnome-print.

Misc info

Raph Levien has acquired several patents on various technologies over the years. What he decided to do with them (link to ZDNet article) is somewhat revolutionary though. More exact information is available on his homepage.
The GNOME website has a year-old interview with Raph.
