Personal info

Tigert's portal page is at tigert.gimp.org. Not much personal there and his Advogato diary has not been updated for a while either. What is known about Tigert is his love for Gimp, the image editor and that he is employed by Ximian.

Contributions

Tigert is probably best-known for his Gnome icons. He is also the author of several Sawfish themes, namely all Helix-sweetpill versions, and numerous other artwork floating around the net. Like for example background patterns.
