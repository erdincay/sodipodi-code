Personal info

Miguel has a linkful homepage at Ximian Inc, a company he helped to found and is working at right now. The homepage itself is at http://primates.helixcode.com/~miguel . It is a good information source not only about Miguel but also GNOME (and UNIX in general).
Miguel has an Advogato entry, but as he also mentions there, his diary is kept on his real homepage (see above).
Miguel is a member of the GNOME Foundation Board of Directors.

Contributions
