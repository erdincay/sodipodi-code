A poster made to advertise a summercamp conducted by the Asociacion Xuvenil Itaca (http://www.federacionitaca.org). I did not see any other poster in the Sodipodi galleries, so it may be interesting as an example. Designed to be black and white photocopied on DIN-A3 (or larger) coloured paper, and cut along the diagonal, which doubles the amount of posters (thin economy) and provides an original poster shape.
